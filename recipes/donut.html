Homemade Donuts

Crispy and Creamy Donuts

This recipe is sourced from 'All Recipes' website

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of warm water
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 1/3 cup of shortening

Steps

  1. Sprinkle the yeast over warm water
  2. Mix in large bowl
  3. Trun dough into a floured surface
  4. Heat Oil
  5. Enjoy!
