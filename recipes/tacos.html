Homemade Beef Tacos

Midwest Beef Tacos

This recipe was sourced from the website 'Cooking in the Midwest'

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 TBSP minced garlic
  • 1 TSP each of salt and pepper
  • 1/2 TSP cayenne
  • 4 TSP chili powder
  • 2 TSP each of cumin and dried cilantro

Steps

  1. Thaw Beef
  2. Chop onions
  3. Measure Seasoning
  4. Heat large skillet
  5. Season cooked meat
  6. Wait 20 minutes
  7. Build Tacos
